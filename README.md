# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

A retail analytics project analyzing 3,900 transactions to uncover customer spending patterns, product preferences, and loyalty behavior.

---

## Tools Used

- **Python** — Data cleaning and preparation
- **PostgreSQL** — Business queries and segmentation
- **Power BI** — Interactive dashboard

---

## What Was Done

**Data Preparation**
Cleaned raw data, handled missing review ratings, standardized column names, and engineered age group and purchase frequency features.

**SQL Analysis**
Ran 10 business queries covering revenue by gender, customer segmentation, top products, discount behavior, and subscription impact.

**Dashboard**
Built an interactive Power BI dashboard with KPIs, category breakdowns, and age group revenue charts.

---

## Key Findings

- Male customers contributed roughly twice the revenue of female customers
- 80% of customers fall into the Loyal segment
- Only 27% of customers are subscribed, despite similar average spend to non-subscribers
- Express shipping users spend slightly more on average
- Hats, sneakers, and coats show the highest discount dependency (~50%)

---

## Recommendations

- Launch a subscription drive targeting the 73% non-subscriber base
- Introduce loyalty rewards to retain and grow the loyal customer segment
- Review discount strategy for high-dependency products to protect margins
- Focus marketing on young adults, who are the highest revenue age group
